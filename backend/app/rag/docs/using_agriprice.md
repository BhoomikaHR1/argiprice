# Using the AgriPrice App

AgriPrice is an AI-powered agricultural market intelligence platform for Karnataka farmers.

## What you can do
- Live Prices: see today's modal, min, and max prices for crops across APMC mandis.
- Market Trends: view historical price movement to time your sale.
- AI Prediction: get a sell/hold recommendation and a suggested mandi for a crop and quantity.
- Crops: browse crop details, varieties, MSP, season, and storage info.
- APMC Markets: find markets, including the nearest mandi to your location.
- Government Schemes: discover central and state schemes relevant to your crops.
- MSP: check the latest Minimum Support Prices by crop and category.
- Weather: get district-level and location-based weather for crop planning.
- Joint Community: connect with other farmers for group/bulk selling.

## Getting the best out of AgriPrice
- Set your district so prices and weather are local to you.
- Save your crops to get focused price and scheme updates.
- Use AI Prediction before selling large quantities to pick the best market and timing.
- Compare prices across nearby mandis on the Live Prices page before transporting produce.

## Languages
The app supports English and Kannada. Set your preferred language in your profile.
