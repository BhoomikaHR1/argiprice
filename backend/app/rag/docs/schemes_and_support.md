# Government Schemes & Support for Farmers

## PM-KISAN
PM-KISAN gives ₹6,000 per year to eligible farmer families in three equal instalments of
₹2,000, paid directly into the bank account. Register with land records and Aadhaar through
the PM-KISAN portal or a Common Service Centre (CSC).

## Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
Crop insurance against losses from natural calamities, pests, and disease. Farmers pay a
low premium (2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi, 5% for commercial/horticultural crops); the
government subsidises the rest. Enrol at your bank, CSC, or insurance agent before the
cut-off date for the season.

## Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
Provides short-term crop loans at subsidised interest. Timely repayment can bring the
effective interest rate down to around 4%. Apply at any bank with land records and ID.

## Soil Health Card
Free soil testing that recommends the right fertiliser dose for your land, reducing input
cost and improving yield. Request through the local agriculture department.

## Karnataka State schemes
- Raitha Siri / Raitha Belaku: input support and incentives for specific crops like millets.
- Krishi Bhagya: support for farm ponds and rainwater harvesting in rain-fed areas.
- Subsidies on drip/sprinkler irrigation, farm machinery, and polyhouses.
Check the Karnataka Department of Agriculture or your Raitha Samparka Kendra for current
schemes, eligibility, and application windows.

## Documents commonly needed
Aadhaar, bank passbook, RTC/Pahani land record, passport photo, and crop/season details.
